MT4 Indicators Collection - Free MetaTrader 4 Trading Tools

Enhance your forex and stock trading with these powerful, free MT4 indicators. Each tool is designed for MetaTrader 4, offering customizable features like alerts, multi-timeframe support, and easy downloads. Perfect for beginners and pros alike.

Averages Indicator MT4

Averages Indicator MT4 is an all-in-one moving average indicator for MetaTrader 4 that supports various formulas and price types for customizable chart analysis. It offers alerts, emails, and notifications, and can be downloaded for free with limitations to demo accounts.

Patterns indicator MT4

Patterns indicator MT4 is a MetaTrader MT4 and MT5 expert advisor that searches for various trading patterns and draws their future projection on the chart. It supports patterns like Gartley, ABCD, and Wolf Waves, and can be downloaded for free with limitations for demo accounts.

Demark + Channels indicator MT4

Demark + Channels indicator MT4 is a MetaTrader tool that plots DeMark lines, channels, and trend lines, includes three targets with the third at 1.62 of the inner extremum, and cancels signals when price closes within the channel. It is compatible with most brokers, offers a one-time payment with free life updates, and is available for free download limited to demo accounts.

Regression Channel indicator MT4

The Regression Channel indicator MT4 is a MetaTrader tool for trading forex, stocks, indices, and futures, consisting of two parallel lines equidistant from the linear regression trend based on maximum close price deviation (Kstd). It offers three channel types: linear, polynomial, and parabolic, and can be downloaded for free with limitations to demo accounts.

3 Lines Support / Resistance indicator MT4

This Support / Resistance indicator for MT4 displays SR price levels for any chosen timeframe and possible future SR lines dynamically. When the price closes at a support or resistance level, the line is erased and replaced by the new current level.

Trend Trader indicator MT4

The MetaTrader 4 Trend Trader indicator combines MACD, PSAR, Stochastic, and a moving average to identify and trade trends. It draws the price channel, spots swings, identifies candle types, displays round numbers, and shows trend signals with arrows and candle colors across timeframes.

ATR Candles indicator MT4

The ATR Candles indicator for MT4 repaints candle colors based on ATR settings to match volatility. It features two adjustable settings for ATR period and multiplier, and is available for free download with limitations to demo accounts.

MTF Candles indicator MT4

This Metatrader 4 indicator draws any higher timeframe candles on the current trading chart, eliminating the need to change timeframes to find daily start, previous days highs/lows, and displaying the current candle to show the day's trend.

Heiken Ashi & Bands indicator MT4

This Heiken Ashi indicator for MetaTrader has been improved with a moving average band to determine potential price reversal or breakout levels. It helps traders identify trend strength and avoid noise in consolidation zones.

ZigZag Channel indicator MT4

The ZigZag Channel indicator combines the ZigZag indicator with a price channel drawn by zigzag highs and lows to help traders identify entry points for trend or breakout trades. It offers customizable channel appearance and alerts for price breakouts.

StairSteps indicator MT4

The StairSteps indicator is a consolidation zone indicator that draws CZ on the chart and alerts traders on breakouts, regardless of trend. It is available for free download, limited to demo accounts, and can be automated with the StairSteps EA.

Stochastic Divergence indicator MT4

The Stochastic Divergence indicator MT4 is a custom technical indicator that adds a divergence component to the Stochastic Oscillator, plotting lines between peaks and dips on the oscillator and price chart to provide mean reversal signals. It identifies regular and hidden divergences to signal potential trend reversals or continuations, aiding forex traders in entry and exit decisions.

Support / Resistance indicator MT4

The Support / Resistance indicator MT4 is a MetaTrader tool that automatically draws support and resistance lines to help traders identify market sentiment, trend direction and strength, and optimal entry and exit points for trades. It can be downloaded for free and is compatible with most brokers.

MACD Multi TimeFrame Panel indicator MT4

The MACD Multi TimeFrame Panel indicator for MT4 displays MACD for any timeframe on the current chart in a small panel, eliminating the need for the default indicator and allowing more space for the price chart. It is useful for traders using MACD trend trading strategies to detect trends and possible reversals, with customizable settings for multiple timeframes and alert options.

Moving Average Candles indicator MT4

The Single Moving Average Candles indicator for MetaTrader colors candles based on their position relative to a moving average, allowing traders to clear the chart and better observe price action. It supports various moving average types and lengths, and can be used to detect trends or spot entries and exits.
